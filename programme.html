Programme

The day will be filled with one-on-one conversations, which will be scheduled for each student. There will also be lunch included. In the registration form, you can indicate what time slots would be suitable for you so we can make a schedule that will fit you! We will try to schedule as much company talks as you are interested in, however, we can not guarantee you will be scheduled with all your choices.

Throughout the day, there will also be talks about pursuing a career in education or academia. The day will end with a keynote speech by an alumnus of Applied Mathematics. It is also possible to sign up for these talks in the registration form.

Career perspectives in Education
10:30 to 11:15 Career perspectives in Academia by Kees Vuik
17:00 to 17:45 Career Perspectives in Industry by Irine Gaasbeek

Irine Gaasbeek is the country managing director of Accenture Netherlands. In this role, she is in charge of Accenture's business operations in our country, with a specific focus on growth through innovation and digital transformation.

Although her background mainly lies in system implementation and IT transformations, Irine is, above all, motivated by relationships. Central to her work is the belief that building valuable relationships is based on honesty, transparency and integrity. That means delivering on promised commitments, no matter how complex or innovative the challenge.
