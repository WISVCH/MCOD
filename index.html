Career Orientation Days - 28/04/2021 & 29/04/2021

The event previously known as the Mathematical Career Orientation Day has been expanded! This year, there will also be a separate day for Computer Science students. Donâ€™t worry, the event as you might already know it will remain completely untouched. On the 28th of April, the day for Applied Mathematics students will take place and the 29th of April will be the day for Computer Science students.

The Career Orientation Days are aimed at students who are currently studying Applied Mathematics or Computer Science & Engineering and are eager to find out what they can do after finishing their studies. During the event, you will get the opportunity to have one-on-one talks with graduates of your study who are currently working at the participating companies. This can be just an orientating conversation, but the interview may also directly lead to a job, internship or graduation project!

Throughout the day, there will also be talks about pursuing a career in education or academia. The day will end with a keynote speech by an alumnus of Applied Mathematics. It is also possible to sign up for these talks in the registration form, which will be available later.

Updates

Stay up to date by following our Facebook Event. On this page we will announce the companies that will attend the Mathematical Career Orientation Day. These companies will each get a profile on our website with more information. For every company announcement, there will be a different Facebook message.

To let us know that you are interested, please fill in this form. You will immediately receive an email, when the registrations open.

COVID-19 Update

Due to the measures regarding COVID-19, it is not yet certain whether the Career Orientation Days will take place online or in person. However, if you will not be able to attend the event in person, we will make sure you will be able to join the event from home. You will be updated accordingly once more information is available.
